About

Bits and Blocks Village

At Bits and Blocks we showcase networks built on blockchain technology. Applications controlling assets with distributed transactions are illustrated. Mandarin speakers and English speakers are welcome to complete a free blockchain workshop for beginners in the Bits and Blocks village.

For advanced hackers, Bits and Blocks features detailed guidance on the Schnorr signature algorithm, the cryptographic Merkle tree, and confidential transactions.

Social games and fun include our blockchain challenge awards participants according to level reached in a gamified guide of steps. Come to our blockchain party, where Bits and Blocks hosts a social meeting, movie film, and refreshments.

Speaking

Call for Presentations

To submit a proposal to present, send to staff@bitsblocks.org:

  • Type of proposal
    1. Speech
    2. Showcase
    3. Workshop
    4. Other type
  • Topic of proposal
  • Presentation title
  • Presentation language
  • Presenter name (or pseudoname)
  • Presenter contact (email, weixing)
  • Presentation abstract (or outline)
  • Presentation duration (minutes)
  • Extra information (including cospeaker, schedule, etcetera)
  • Presenter biography (background, experience, and projects)

CFP Deadline: 30 April 2019

The Bits and Blocks review board will select and award acceptance to presenters soon after the CFP deadline. Accepted presenters must confirm their participation after that.

Bits and Blocks staff are working to help presenters with benefits, which may include free entrance or visa invitation letters on a case by case basis.
