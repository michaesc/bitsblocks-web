Bits and Blocks : Defcon Village  


31 May - 02 May 2019

  At Bits and Blocks we showcase networks built on blockchain technology. Applications controlling assets with distributed transactions are examined. Mandarin speakers and English speakers are welcome to experience a free blockchain workshop for beginners in the Bits and Blocks village.

  For advanced hackers, Bits and Blocks features detailed guidance on the Schnorr signature algorithm, the cryptographic Merkle tree, and confidential transactions. Hardware projects showcase developments in device driven blockchain applications.

  Social games and fun include our blockchain challenge awards participants according to level reached in a gamified guide of steps. Come to our blockchain party, where Bits and Blocks hosts a social meeting, movie film, and refreshments.
